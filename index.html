Services & Tools

Dashboard & Data Visualization

Excel and/or Power Bi, merged with a good Story telling, are able to act like a book or a map filled with dynamic charts with the purpose of reaching the best understanding of our data.

Descriptive Analysis

Describing or summarizing a set of data. It is popular for its ability to generate accessible insights from otherwise uninterpreted data. It draws insights solely from past data, by manipulating in ways that make it more meaningful.

Diagnostic Analysis

Using the insights found from descriptive analytics to find the causes of outcomes. It creates more connections between data and identifies patterns of behavior.

Prescriptive Analysis

Analyzing data and providing instant recommendations on how to optimize business practices to suit multiple predicted outcomes. It takes the “what we know” (data), comprehensively understands that data to predict what could happen, and suggests the best steps forward based on informed simulations.

Healthcare Analysis

Utilizing data-driven approaches to examine healthcare-related information, identifying trends, patterns, and insights to enhance patient care, optimize operational efficiency, and improve overall healthcare outcomes. This involves analyzing various aspects of healthcare data, including patient demographics, treatment outcomes, resource allocation, and medical costs, to inform decision-making processes and drive continuous improvement in the healthcare industry.
